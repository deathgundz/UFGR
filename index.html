Phase 1

-To answer that question, I made a special quiz, and the winner of it will get the title of “the coolest character in modern fighting games”. In this quiz, you will be going through two phases. This is phase 1. Since a powerful first impression goes hand in hand with being cool, you will have 10 seconds to rate every character from ten different games. The top 3 of each game will move forward to the next phase. Make sure not to give high scores at the start, since the first three who get 10 will be the ones to qualify. (the game has to be released between 2020-2024 to be put in here)

Phase 2

Since this is a fighting games quiz, the top 32 characters will be competing in one single elimination bracket, and you will choose the winner by just your pure imagination.
